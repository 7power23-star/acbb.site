Atrophaneura varuna astorion

Authority: Westwood, 1842

Common Name

Common Batwing

Taxonomic Position

  • Family: Papilionidae
  • Genus: Atrophaneura
  • Species: A. varuna astorion

Distribution

Recorded from forested regions of Bangladesh, particularly in the northeastern and southeastern hill forests.

Remarks

This subspecies is a characteristic swallowtail of forest habitats and is associated with Aristolochiaceae host plants.
